Community Slang Terms

Based
Being yourself without caring what others think. Authentic and confident.
Ex: "That's a based take on the situation."
Cope
To make excuses or rationalize something negative. Dealing with disappointment.
Ex: "Stop coping about your loss, accept it!"
Salty
Being upset, bitter, or frustrated about something. Acting grumpy.
Ex: "Don't be so salty about losing the game!"
Vibe
The feeling or atmosphere of something. The overall energy.
Ex: "I'm getting good vibes from this."
Mid
Something that is mediocre or not very good. Average quality.
Ex: "That movie was mid, not worth watching."
