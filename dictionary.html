Slang Dictionary

Terms

based

being yourself without caring what others think. authentic and confident.

ex: "that's a based take"

cope

to make excuses or rationalize something negative.

ex: "stop coping about the loss"

salty

being upset, bitter, or frustrated about something.

ex: "don't be so salty about losing"

vibe

the feeling or atmosphere of something. the overall energy.

ex: "good vibes from this"

mid

something that is mediocre or not very good.

ex: "that movie was mid"
